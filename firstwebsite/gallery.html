Favorite Foods

Chicken and Lamb Platter

Chicken and Lamb platter is basically a platter consisting of shreded or cut into small pieces of chicken and lamb tenders. The meat is yogurt-marinated over night and then seasoned to perfection. After that it's cooked in the oven. Then the chicken and lamb in plated over some yellow rice and shreded veggies if preffered. It's finished off with a special white sauce and spicy sauce if preffered as well.

Chicken Biryani

Biryani is a mixed rice dish originating among the Muslims of the Indian subcontinent. It is made with spices, rice, and meat, or eggs or vegetables such as potatoes in certain regional varieties. Biryani is one of the most popular dishes in South Asia, as well as among the diaspora from the region. The ingredients used are Mutton, chicken, beef, egg, potato and other vegetables.

Butter Chicken

Murgh makhani, anglicised as butter chicken, is a curry of chicken in a spiced tomato and butter sauce. It originated in the Indian subcontinent as a curry. It is similar to chicken tikka masala, which uses a tomato paste. You can have it with rice or Indian flat breads like naan, kulcha, chapati, roti, etc. If you don't have anything, just place it atop a piece of store-bought bread and enjoy! Butter chicken tastes joyously delicious in all and every form.

Loaded Fries

Cheese fries or cheesy chips is a dish consisting of French fries covered in cheese, with the possible addition of various other toppings. Cheese fries are generally served as a lunch- or dinner-time meal. They can be found in fast-food locations, diners, and grills around the globe. It's one of my go to cheat meal when I am really hungry. It tastes the best while watching a movie or show.

Favorite Pets

Cat

The cat is a domestic species of small carnivorous mammal. It is the only domesticated species in the family Felidae and is often referred to as the domestic cat to distinguish it from the wild members of the family.

Dog

The dog or domestic dog is a domesticated descendant of the wolf which is characterized by an upturning tail. The dog derived from an ancient, extinct wolf, and the modern grey wolf is the dog's nearest living relative.

Bird

Bird is a micromobility company based in Santa Monica, California. Founded in September 2017 Bird operates shared electric scooters in over 100 cities in Europe, the Middle East, and North America with 10 million rides in its first year of operation.

Horse

The horse is a domesticated one-toed hoofed mammal. It belongs to the taxonomic family Equidae and is one of two extant subspecies of Equus ferus. The horse has evolved over the past 45 to 55 million years from a small multi-toed creature, Eohippus, into the large, single-toed animal of today.
